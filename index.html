Diabetic Retinopathy Prediction

Diabetic Retinopathy is a human eye disease that causes damage to the retina and may eventually lead to blindness. Early detection through modern technology is crucial to prevent complete blindness. Many physical tests, such as visual acuity tests and pupil dilation, are conducted for diagnosis. This system offers an automated, deep-learning-powered solution to enhance detection accuracy.
